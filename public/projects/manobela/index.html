Manobela

A phone-first driver monitoring system that detects risky driving behavior in real time and helps prevent accidents before they happen.

Why This Matters

Manobela was built around one clear fact from the project research: most road crashes are linked to human behavior, especially distraction and fatigue. Instead of requiring expensive in-car systems, Manobela runs on a smartphone so more drivers can access safety support.

  • Road crashes cause around 1.3 million deaths globally each year.
  • Human error is linked to the large majority of crashes.
  • In the Philippines, unsafe driver behavior remains a major cause of fatalities.

The Simple Idea

Keep the experience practical: monitor behavior live, alert the driver immediately, and provide post-drive insights for long-term improvement. No extra hardware is required.

What Manobela Detects

  • Eye closure and yawning for fatigue signals.
  • Head pose and gaze direction for attention tracking.
  • Phone usage events for distraction detection.
  • Face presence and session quality indicators.

How The App Helps Drivers

  • Real-time alerts through visual, audio, and haptic feedback.
  • Upload-and-analyze mode for recorded sessions.
  • Insights dashboard with trend and session summaries.
  • Map-linked sessions for trip-level context.
  • Privacy-first defaults with local processing and local storage.
