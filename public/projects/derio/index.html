Derio

Personal finance manager that structures transactions, budgets, and monthly summaries into clear decision views.

Overview

Derio was designed as a practical personal finance tracker where categorization and monthly analysis stay understandable without complex setup.

What Was Built

  • Transaction capture and budget allocation workflow.
  • Monthly summaries that surface spending behavior and trends.
  • Data structures optimized for readable reporting and maintenance.

Outcome

The project established a stable data flow that supports realistic finance scenarios while remaining simple to extend.
