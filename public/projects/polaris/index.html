Polaris

Logistics prototype that models shipment flows and supply-chain checkpoints with a domain-oriented structure.

Overview

Polaris translates logistics terminology into usable system entities so shipment movement and checkpoints can be simulated with clear lifecycle states.

What Was Built

  • Shipment and checkpoint models aligned to common supply-chain operations.
  • Workflow representation for transitions, handoffs, and tracking status.
  • Backend logic prepared for expansion into richer analytics and optimization.

Outcome

The prototype validated domain mapping decisions and made it easier to discuss logistics workflows in code.
