pagbutlakUPV

The official website and publishing platform for Pagbutlak UPV.

Overview

pagbutlakUPV gives the student and community publication of the College of Arts and Sciences at UP Visayas a dedicated home for stories, announcements, and editorial content. The platform combines a public reading experience with a structured administration workflow.

Key Capabilities

  • CMS-managed articles, pages, authors, categories, and media.
  • Editorial administration and live content preview.
  • Search, nested navigation, redirects, forms, and SEO metadata.
  • Generated sitemap support for discoverable publication content.
  • PostgreSQL persistence with local and Docker development workflows.

Technical Approach

The site is built with Next.js and Payload CMS in a single TypeScript application. Payload owns editorial data and administration, while the frontend renders public content, search, forms, and SEO-aware routes. Automated integration and browser tests support changes to both sides of the platform.

Stack

Next.js, TypeScript, React, Payload CMS, PostgreSQL, Tailwind CSS, Vitest, and Playwright.
